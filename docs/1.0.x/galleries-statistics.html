Viewing the Gallery Statistics

The gallery statistics interface allows you to get a quick overview of the most clicked galleries, sponsors and categories. To view the gallery statistics access the Galleries > Gallery Statistics function from the control panel menu. When this page loads you will find a drop down selection field that will allow you to pick the statistic you want to view. Each of those is described below.
    Most Clicked Categories
    This will display all of your categories with a sum total of the number of clicks the galleries in that category have received

    Most Clicked Sponsors
    This will display all of your sponsors with a sum total of the number of clicks the galleries assigned to that sponsor have received

    Most Clicked Galleries
    This will display the top 30 most clicked galleries

    Most Productive Categories (Used counter)
    This will display all of your categories with a sum total of their productivity (clicks/used_counter)

    Most Productive Sponsors (Used counter)
    This will display all of your sponsors with a sum total of their productivity (clicks/used_counter)

    Most Productive Galleries (Used counter)
    This will display the top 30 most productive galleries (clicks/used_counter)

    Most Productive Categories (Build counter)
    This will display all of your categories with a sum total of their productivity (clicks/build_counter)

    Most Productive Sponsors (Build counter)
    This will display all of your sponsors with a sum total of their productivity (clicks/build_counter)

    Most Productive Galleries (Build counter)
    This will display the top 30 most productive galleries (clicks/build_counter)
After you select the statistic you want to view, press the View Stats button. TGPX will load the information and display it below the drop down selection field.
